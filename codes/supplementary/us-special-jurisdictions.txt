================ Notes ================
=======================================

Includes US states and territories as an identification aid. Abbreviations when available, but those aren't unique.

Libraries *vastly* outnumber any other component. Perhaps as many as 17,000 public libraries in United States if branch locations are included. Still as many as 9000 if those are excluded. Fewer than a dozen field police departments, possibly only LOC. That sublist could potentially be used for non2x codes, but would need to be supplemented with college codes to contain all major libraries in the US correctly.

Hospitals are estimated at 4000ish for the US, so this is going to just barely fit.
